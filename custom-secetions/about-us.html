The Beginning of Something Special

Velura was created out of a deep love for babies and a commitment to providing them with the safest, most gentle care. Seeing parents struggle to find truly natural baby products inspired us to make a difference. We believed there was a better way—one that combined the purity of nature with the expertise of pediatric care to nurture delicate skin in the best possible way.

From Home Lab to Trusted Brand

What started as a small-scale endeavour in Dr. J.K. Pushkar’s home lab has become a trusted name in baby care. Every product is carefully crafted with natural ingredients, chosen for their safety and effectiveness. Velura’s journey is a testament to the dedication of a paediatrician who combined medical expertise with a genuine love for children. Each formula is created to nurture and protect, making every parent feel confident in the choices they make for their little ones.

Nature’s Best for Baby’s Skin

At the heart of Velura is a commitment to using only the purest natural ingredients. From soothing botanical extracts to nourishing oils, every component is picked to provide gentle, effective care. Velura’s products are dermatologically tested and free from harmful chemicals, being true to safety and sustainability. Our products offer every baby the best nature has to offer, wrapped in a touch of loving care.

A Future of Compassionate Innovation

Looking ahead, we are excited about the future. As Velura continues to evolve, we remain true to our founding principles. We are dedicated to innovation, sustainability, and community support. Our goal is to be a driving force in the baby care industry, offering parents the safest, most effective, and most natural products available.
